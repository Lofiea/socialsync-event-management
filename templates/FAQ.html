Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the party start?

The party starts at 8:00 PM and goes until late!

Is there an age restriction?

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to enter.

Can I bring a plus one?

Yes! Each guest is allowed to bring one plus one.

What is the dress code?

Dress to impress! No casual wear such as flip-flops or tank tops.
