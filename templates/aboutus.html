Our Mission

Our website gives users an opportunity to find or host parties/events in their region. The website has built-in filters that allow users to find the right party, from private to public. Our subscription service offers benefits like discounts, fast passes, and the option to choose the music at select events. For your safety and peace of mind, we require the user to provide a government-issued ID for identity verification. Plus, the ticket is sent to you in a QR code (after purchase) so you can just show, scan, and enter.

Our Values

  • Safety First: We prioritize the safety and security of our users with verified IDs and secure ticketing.
  • User-Centric: Our platform is designed to make event discovery and hosting seamless and enjoyable.
  • Innovation: We constantly innovate to provide unique features like music selection and fast passes.
  • Community: We believe in bringing people together to celebrate life's moments.
