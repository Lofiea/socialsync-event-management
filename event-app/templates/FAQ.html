Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create am event on SocialSync?

Simply sign up, navigate to the "Host Event" section, and fill in the details of your event.

Can I cancel or refund my ticket?

Refund policies vary by event. Check the event page or contact the organizer for specific details.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

Can I edit my event details after publishing?

Yes, you can edit event details by going to your dashboard and selecting the event you want to modify.
